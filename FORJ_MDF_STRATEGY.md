# THE MDF STRATEGY

2026-07-18. Internal. Grounded in a real filled-in AWS MDF plan (`Alto_MDF_Marketing_Plan.xlsx`)
and AWS's own activity taxonomy, then attacked by a compliance review. Full working output in the
session task file; this is the decision layer.

---

## THE THESIS

**MDF is a qualification filter. It is not a discount, and treating it as one destroys the price.**

A partner who can name their tier, their wallet balance and their wallet expiry date inside one phone
call has an AWS practice real enough to buy a Desk block. A partner who cannot is either too early or
not serious, and the MDF question surfaces that in ninety seconds without Forj ever appearing to
qualify them.

**The second-order value is larger than the money, and it is the actual unlock: MDF shortens the
approval chain.**

> 300,000 SEK at a 30-person consultancy is a CEO decision about the year.
> 150,000 SEK net, drawn against a fund that was already earned and expires unused, is a sales
> director's decision.

Approval chain length is what kills Forj's deals at this stage, not price.

Third: being the named agency on a partner's plan is the only route Forj has to a Nordic AWS partner
marketing manager, and there is no Nordic agency in AWS's Agency Connect catalog competing for that
position.

**Filter first. Approval shortener second. Wedge third. Discount never.** Price against the partner's
own budget. Every krona of reimbursement is the partner's upside, disclosed late, never load-bearing.

## THE MENU

The Alloy subscription never appears on a plan, an invoice or a claim. USD at 10.5 SEK.

### Lead with these three, as one machine

**1. Telemarketing. The Desk.** LIVE.
Plan line: *"Partner-led outbound telemarketing campaign, third-party agency, [N] named AWS-target
accounts in Sweden, delivering qualified AWS opportunities registered in ACE."*
300,000 SEK per 3-month block. Request at $32,000 with $16,000 MDF.
Proof: dated pre-activity target list and ICP criteria, dispositioned call log, meetings held with
dated briefs, **ACE opportunity IDs created inside the window**, itemised invoice separate from software.

> **BUILD GAP, AND IT IS NUMBER ONE.** The dialer and Telavox capture are parked, so the flagship
> fundable line currently has the weakest evidence trail Forj produces. Mandatory timestamped
> disposition logging in Alloy for every account in a Desk block, before any telemarketing line is
> claimed. Without it the claim is undefendable.

**2. Gated content. The segment report.** LIVE. Best ratio on the board.
A partner-branded report off the corpus, aggregate and anonymous: 85,693 companies cloud-read, AWS
ecosystem counts, funded-play match rates by segment. Nobody else in the Nordics has this data.
$6,000 to $15,000. Founder hours low, the corpus already exists.

> **The strategic bonus is bigger than the fee.** A download gate collects contact data DIRECTLY,
> which is Article 13, not Article 14. This is the cleanest first-party lead route Forj has, and it
> progressively replaces the provenance-compromised contact base.

Integrity: never two partners on the same segment in the same year. The invoice describes the
per-partner work, never "production of a report".

**3. Web design. Campaign landing page.** LIVE, proven.
Page, Swedish or Norwegian copy, form wired into the partner's own CRM. The CRM wiring is what beats
a design shop. $3,000 to $9,000.

### Attach, never lead

- **Email campaign as a 1:1 programme.** Fold into the Desk block as one workstream: fewer plan
  lines, fewer claim surfaces. See the email section below.
- **Webinar on-demand.** Record once, gate it, re-cut the intro per partner. No live-date or
  show-rate risk. Pair with the segment report: the session IS the report, presented. $2,000-$5,000.
- **Ungated content.** Solution brief plus messaging house. $2,000-$5,000. Attach only; standalone it
  reads as filler.
- **Customer reference, written.** About the PARTNER's end customer on the partner's letterhead, so
  Alto and Novalo being unnameable is irrelevant. $3,000-$8,000.
  **Contract to "draft delivered approval-ready", never to a publication date.** End-customer legal
  sign-off is the long pole, Forj does not control it, and AWS declines claims where actual
  completion drifts from the approved end date.
- **Customer reference, video: script only.** No camera, no crew, no editor. $2,000-$4,000 with zero
  delivery risk. If they want it filmed, they contract the crew.
- **Webinar live: conditional.** 12 to 20 founder hours for 20 to 60 registrants at maybe 40% show.
  Those same hours on the phones produced 5 to 10 qualified meetings at June's pace. Accept ONLY
  bundled with a Desk block that works the registrant list afterwards. The follow-up is the product;
  the webinar is list generation.

### Refuse, out loud

Paid media. Paid social. Conference. 3P event. Partner event. Filmed video production.

Saying "we do not do that, and here is who should" in front of an AWS-program-fluent buyer buys more
credibility than a padded capability list. On events, take the pre-event target list and the
post-event follow-up desk block, which is the highest-ROI part of any event spend and which almost
every partner does badly. Never take the event line itself.

### Blocked, and it is the highest-value blocked item

**"Other: list purchase and enhancement."** AWS names this in its own eligible-tactic list, which
puts the data layer on the fundable side of the wall where a software subscription sits on the
non-fundable side. Forj still cannot sell it:

1. 97% of contacts came from a Vainu trial API of unverified licence status, so onward supply is a
   licensing question Forj cannot currently answer.
2. Selling a list to an Alloy licence holder is the same asset sold twice, once fundable.

**No list line to any Alloy customer, ever. No list line at all until the Vainu position is resolved.**

## THE EMAIL ANSWER

The category is fundable. The word is the problem.

Forj's standing rule is no mass email: Smith drafts, a human sends from their own mailbox, per
account. That is operationally closer to telemarketing than to a campaign, and putting it on a plan
line as "Email Campaign" risks a mismatch between the activity approved and the activity delivered,
which is a primary rejection cause.

**Sell it as a workstream inside the Desk's telemarketing line, not as its own plan line.** Reserve
genuine Email Campaign work for cases where the partner supplies the list from their own CRM, because
then the data provenance and the Article 14 duty are theirs and the outreach carries their brand
lawfully.

The exposure to be explicit about: under an MDF-funded email activity the outreach carries the
PARTNER's brand. The Article 14 risk lands on them, not Forj. That conversation happens deliberately,
in advance, or it happens badly later.

## THE COMPLIANCE LINE

- Two contracts, always. Software on the software invoice at real price. Campaign block separate.
  Software is never claimed fundable and never bundled into a funded activity.
- Never claim an AWS partnership status, tier or endorsement Forj does not hold. Forj is the agency;
  the partner is the claimant.
- Activity delivered must match activity approved, in name and in substance.
- Timing is mechanical: submit at least 2 weeks before activity start, claim within 30 days of
  completion. Both are rejection causes, and both are calendar items, not judgement calls.
- No per-meeting or per-lead price in any artifact, including the plan and the call script.

2. **Resolve the Vainu trial licence.** It blocks the list line, it is a renewal-conversation

3. **Forj's own AWS standing.** Activate Founders unclaimed, no competency, Marketplace and FTR path
   not started, PC migration lapsed 30 June. When a vPMM asks who Forj is, the answer matters.

## THE ONE MOVE

Ask both renewal customers one question: **"What is your MDF wallet balance and when does it expire?"**

It qualifies them, it surfaces money that is already theirs and is quietly expiring, and it opens the
plan conversation without Forj selling anything. If the answer is a number and a date, there is a
Desk block available on a short approval chain. If the answer is a blank look, that is the finding.
