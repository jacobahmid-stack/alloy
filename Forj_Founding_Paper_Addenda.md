# Founding paper addenda

2026-07-18. Two clauses to attach to every new founding agreement and to the Alto/Novalo annual paper. Both live on the SOFTWARE contract (never the campaign invoice, per the two-contract rule). No em dashes. Swedish version is the signing text; English is the working copy.

## Clause 1: Nordic expansion markets (the reserved add-on SKU)

Purpose: reserve the market add-on in terms NOW so selling it later needs no new paper. Activates the "market add-ons 1,500-2,500 SEK/mo class" line already in the signed pricing strategy. Norway and Finland are loaded and cloud-read as of 2026-07-18, so the SKU is real, not speculative.

### English (working copy)

**Expansion markets.** The subscription covers the Customer's home market (Sweden). Additional Nordic markets (Norway, Finland, Denmark, Estonia) can be activated as add-on markets at 1 900 SEK per market per month on the software invoice, as each market becomes available in Alloy. An activated market includes that market's company library, cloud and technology reads, funded-play matching and signal feeds, and decision-maker data where available. During the founding period, expansion markets are included at no charge; from the first renewal after the founding rate ends, activated markets are invoiced at the then-current add-on rate, capped by the same annual increase cap as the subscription. The Customer chooses which markets are active and can deactivate a market at any quarter boundary.

### Svenska (avtalstext)

**Tillaggsmarknader.** Abonnemanget omfattar kundens hemmamarknad (Sverige). Ytterligare nordiska marknader (Norge, Finland, Danmark, Estland) kan aktiveras som tillaggsmarknader for 1 900 SEK per marknad och manad pa mjukvarufakturan, i takt med att respektive marknad blir tillganglig i Alloy. En aktiverad marknad omfattar marknadens foretagsbibliotek, moln- och tekniklasningar, matchning mot finansierade program och signalfloden, samt beslutsfattardata dar sadan finns. Under grundarperioden ingar tillaggsmarknader utan kostnad; fran forsta fornyelsen efter att grundarpriset upphort faktureras aktiverade marknader till vid var tid gallande tillaggspris, med samma arliga hojningstak som abonnemanget. Kunden valjer vilka marknader som ar aktiva och kan avaktivera en marknad vid varje kvartalsskifte.

Notes for Jacob:
- 1 900 SEK sits inside the reserved 1 500-2 500 corridor. Held per market, per month, flat. No per-contact metering (anti-token spine).
- "Where available" covers the honest state: SE has contacts today; NO/FI contacts are being built. Never promise contact counts in paper.
- Founding tenants get markets free now = a visible, finite founding benefit that converts to NRR at renewal, exactly like territory activation.

## Clause 2: Right to learn (the flywheel clause)

Purpose: without this, every Desk campaign and every tenant workspace builds the customer's asset only, and Forj's data flywheel runs on goodwill. This is the single most transferable instruction from the AI-native services playbook. Goes into the Desk engagement letter AND the software terms. Must be in the Novalo annual paper at the same sitting as the IP quitclaim.

### English (working copy)

**Learning from the work.** Forj may use insights derived from performing the services (such as which company profiles respond, which plays convert, which processes stall, and aggregate outcome statistics) to improve its models, scoring, playbooks and platform, provided that (a) the Customer's confidential information, customer lists, prices and end-customer personal data are never disclosed to any third party or other customer in identifiable form, (b) nothing that identifies the Customer or the Customer's end customers is used in any other customer's workspace or in marketing without written consent, and (c) all personal data handling remains within Forj's published privacy terms. What Forj learns in aggregate belongs to Forj; the Customer's data belongs to the Customer and is exportable in full at any time.

### Svenska (avtalstext)

**Larande fran arbetet.** Forj far anvanda insikter fran utforandet av tjansterna (till exempel vilka foretagsprofiler som svarar, vilka spel som konverterar, var processer stannar, samt aggregerad utfallsstatistik) for att forbattra sina modeller, sin poangsattning, sina spelbocker och sin plattform, forutsatt att (a) kundens konfidentiella information, kundlistor, priser och slutkunders personuppgifter aldrig lamnas ut till tredje part eller annan kund i identifierbar form, (b) inget som identifierar kunden eller kundens slutkunder anvands i annan kunds arbetsyta eller i marknadsforing utan skriftligt samtycke, och (c) all personuppgiftsbehandling sker inom Forjs publicerade integritetsvillkor. Det Forj lar sig pa aggregerad niva tillhor Forj; kundens data tillhor kunden och kan exporteras i sin helhet nar som helst.

Notes for Jacob:
- The clause trades cleanly: the customer keeps their data and their exportability promise (already on the site); Forj keeps the aggregate learning. This is the standard AI-vendor shape and reads as fair because it is.
- Attach to the Novalo annual paper in the same sitting as the quitclaim (W3 plan). If it is signed nowhere else this month, sign it there.
